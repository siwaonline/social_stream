Facebook
============

Go to https://developers.facebook.com/apps/

- Add a new App
- Add product 'Facebook Login' -> Web
- Skip through the steps

.. figure:: ../Images/screen-fb-settings.jpg
    :width: 500px
    :alt: App Settings

    Image 1

- Click 'Settings' on the Left
- Check that the settings look like Image 1
- Save (bottom right corner)

.. figure:: ../Images/screen-fb-api.jpg
    :width: 500px
    :alt: App

    Image 2

- Click on 'Dashboard'
- Copy the 'App-ID' and the 'App-Secret'
